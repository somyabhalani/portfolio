Thesis
01
Most AI being built today is not intelligence. It is retrieval dressed up as reasoning. The difference matters more than most teams want to admit.
02
Memory is the actual bottleneck. Anyone can generate text. Designing a system that remembers, updates, and forgets information correctly is the difference between a toy and a tool.
03
Most agencies assemble. They pick a template, plug in an API, call it a product. Architecture is the set of decisions that cannot be copied — because they were made specifically for this problem, this constraint, this reality.
